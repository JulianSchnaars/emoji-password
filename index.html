Introduction (1/4)

We study the usability of passwords. In this survey, you will be asked to create a password. Please behave as naturally as possible, so please answer intuitively. There are no correct or wrong answers. As such, there are no “good” and “bad” passwords.

Do not use a password that you already use elsewhere!

To help you do this, imagine you were asked to choose a new password for your primary email account, because the old one has expired.

We will not store any data that can be linked back to you. Neither will we store your password. We are only going to collect some information about it, for example how long it is.

By clicking 'Start' I accept these conditions and agree that cookies will be stored on my device for a limited time.
